About Us

We are Erasmus students documenting our journey in Osijek, Croatia. This blog is our way of sharing experiences, tips, and insights to help future students make the most of their exchange adventure.

Our Mission

Starting an Erasmus journey can be both exciting and overwhelming. We created this blog to make the transition easier for future students by providing practical information, honest experiences, and helpful insights about studying in Osijek.

  • Share authentic student experiences from Osijek
  • Provide practical tips for accommodation, transport, and daily life
  • Help future students prepare for their Erasmus adventure
  • Create a community of international students in Croatia

Whether you're researching potential destinations, preparing for your departure, or already living in Osijek, we hope this blog serves as a valuable resource throughout your Erasmus journey.
