Destination: Osijek

Welcome to Osijek, the vibrant heart of the Slavonia region in Eastern Croatia. As the country's fourth-largest city, Osijek is a hidden gem nestled on the banks of the Drava River.

This page is our guide to the city we'll call home for the next six months, from its historic streets to its student-led culture.

A City of Parks and History

Osijek is known as Croatia's 'greenest city', with over 17 parks and the beautiful Drava river promenade.

Its identity is split between the stunning 18th-century baroque fortress, Tvrƒëa, and a modern, university-driven city center. It's this mix of history and nature that makes it unique.
