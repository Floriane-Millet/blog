Welcome to Osijek

Discover the vibrant heart of Slavonia

Destination: Osijek

Welcome to Osijek, the vibrant heart of the Slavonia region in Eastern Croatia. As the country's fourth-largest city, Osijek is a hidden gem nestled on the banks of the Drava River. This page is our guide to the city we'll call home for the next six months, from its historic streets to its student-led culture.

Osijek at a Glance

Key Information

  • Location: Eastern Croatia, Slavonia Region
  • Population: Approx. 100,000 inhabitants
  • Language: Croatian (English widely spoken in academic circles)
  • Currency: Euro (‚Ç¨)
  • Climate: Continental (hot summers, cold winters)

A City of Parks and History

Osijek is known as Croatia's 'greenest city', with over 17 parks and the beautiful Drava river promenade. Its identity is split between the stunning 18th-century baroque fortress, Tvrƒëa, and a modern, university-driven city center. It's this mix of history and nature that makes it unique.

Our Host: Josip Juraj Strossmayer University (UNIOS)

We will be studying at the Josip Juraj Strossmayer University of Osijek. Founded in 1975, it's a major academic and research hub for the region. We'll be part of a student body of over 15,000, spread across various faculties. We are looking forward to discovering the campus and integrating into this vibrant academic community.
