ESN Osijek - Your Best Friend

Erasmus Student Network (ESN) Osijek is the main organization for international students. They'll be your first point of contact and main social hub.

What ESN Offers:

  • Welcome Week: Orientation activities, campus tours, and meet & greet events
  • Buddy System: Local students assigned to help you settle in
  • Weekly Events: Parties, pub crawls, movie nights, game nights
  • Weekend Trips: Organized excursions to Zagreb, Plitvice Lakes, Split, and more
  • Cultural Nights: International students share food and culture from their countries
  • Language Exchange: Practice Croatian with locals and help them with English

üì± Join ESN Early!

Follow @esnosijek on Instagram and Facebook before you arrive. Get the ESNcard for discounts on travel, restaurants, and events across Europe!

Tips for Making the Most of Your Stay

  • Say Yes to Everything: Especially in the first weeks - this is how you'll make friends!
  • Learn Basic Croatian: Locals appreciate the effort and it helps integration
  • Join Clubs: Sports teams, music groups, volunteer organizations
  • Explore Beyond Osijek: Croatia has amazing diversity - use weekends wisely
  • Document Everything: Photos, videos, journal - you'll want to remember this!
  • Balance is Key: Party, but don't forget why you're here - studies matter too!
  • Be Open-Minded: Embrace cultural differences and new experiences
  • Connect with Locals: Don't just stick with other international students
