On Campus: UNIOS

The university (UNIOS) is spread across the city, with a large, modern campus. Beyond classes, you can get involved with the Student Union (Studentski zbor) which organizes events, or check out cultural happenings at the Academy of Arts and Culture.

The Student "Holy Trinity": Menza, Kava & Tvrƒëa

1. The "Menza" (Student Canteens)

Your most important tool for saving money will be your 'iksica' (X-card), the official Croatian student card. This card gives you access to heavily subsidized meals at student canteens, known as 'Menza'. You can get a full meal for just a few euros.

Popular Menza locations:

  • Restaurant Campus (at the main campus)
  • Restaurant Istarska / Gaudeamus (near the faculties)
