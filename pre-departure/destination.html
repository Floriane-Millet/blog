Welcome to Osijek

Osijek is Croatia's fourth-largest city, located in the eastern Slavonia region on the banks of the Drava River. With a population of around 100,000, it offers the perfect balance between a lively student atmosphere and a relaxed, authentic Croatian lifestyle.

Why Choose Osijek?

Osijek combines rich history, vibrant student culture, and affordable living costs, making it an ideal Erasmus destination. Here's what makes it special:

  • Strong Student Community: With Josip Juraj Strossmayer University hosting thousands of international students, you'll find a welcoming and diverse community.
  • Affordable Living: Compared to western European cities, Osijek offers excellent value for accommodation, food, and entertainment.
  • Rich Culture: From baroque architecture to traditional Croatian festivals, there's always something to explore.
  • Central Location: Easy access to Budapest, Zagreb, Belgrade, and other major cities in the region.
  • Safe Environment: Osijek is known for its low crime rate and friendly, helpful locals.

Language & Communication

The official language is Croatian, but don't worry if you don't speak it! Here's what to know:

  • Many students and young people speak English
  • University courses for Erasmus students are typically in English
  • Learning basic Croatian phrases will be appreciated by locals
  • Free Croatian language courses are often available for Erasmus students

Essential Croatian Phrases
