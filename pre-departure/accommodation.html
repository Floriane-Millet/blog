Student Dormitories (Studentski Dom)

The university offers several student dormitories. They're the most affordable option and great for meeting other international students.

Main Dormitories:

  • Cvjetno Naselje: Largest dorm, close to most faculties
  • Sjenjak: Modern facilities, popular among Erasmus students
  • Vijenac: Smaller, quieter, good for focused study

Practical Tips for Apartment Hunting

  • Start Early: Begin searching 1-2 months before arrival
  • Visit Before Signing: If possible, arrange a video call to see the place
  • Read the Contract: Understand deposit requirements and notice periods
  • Check Location: Proximity to university, shops, and public transport
  • Ask About Bills: Clarify what's included in rent vs. paid separately
  • Meet Roommates: If sharing, try to chat with future roommates first
  • Deposit: Expect to pay 1 month's rent as deposit (returned at end)

üö® Avoid Scams

  • Never send money before seeing the apartment or having a video tour
  • Don't pay full rent in advance for multiple months
  • Always get a signed contract
  • Verify landlord identity and ownership
